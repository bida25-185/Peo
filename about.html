Our Mission

CampusConnect exists to make academic and campus information easy to find, understand, and use. Our mission is to give university students one reliable place where they can access notes, announcements, and updates that support their studies and campus life.

Our Vision

We imagine a campus where no student misses an important update or study resource. CampusConnect aims to be the main digital hub for students in Gaborone, helping them stay informed, engaged, and ready for academic success.

About the Developer

CampusConnect was developed as a student‑centred project to solve a real problem: important information was scattered across WhatsApp groups, social media, and notice boards. This platform brings everything together in one organised, searchable website so students can quickly find what they need, anytime and from anywhere.
