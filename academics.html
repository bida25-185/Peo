Study Tips

Practical advice to help students improve their study habits and manage their time effectively.

  • Create a weekly study schedule and stick to it.
  • Review notes regularly instead of cramming before exams.
  • Use past papers and examples to practise.
  • Study in a quiet space with minimal distractions.
